“You will be defined not just by what you achieve, but by how you survive.” Sheryl Sandberg

Alyssa E. Cleofas believes the motto: "There are no homely women, only careless women."
By convincing those "careless women" they could become beautiful with a little help from the products she took on the giants of the cosmetics world and won.
Starting with little more than a dream, through extraordinary ambition, impeccable taste, perseverance, innovative marketing and hard work,
this creative visionary became the wealthiest self-made woman in Philippines and created a family dynasty that continues today.
Thanks to the romantic, mythical background she created for herself, little was known of Alyssa's childhood life until 2020.
